About Fitverse.ai

Fitverse.ai are leveraging technology for seamless fitness experience – Matching with the right coach, Virtual 1:1 Personal training and connectivity with the trainers. they are journey began with a passion for fitness and a desire to make a positive impact on people's lives. That's why we have built a comprehensive online platform that brings fitness near to the users.
