Will you ship Balash to me?

No, as an early-stage startup, shipping small quantities over long distances is neither cost-effective nor eco-friendly. However, we are currently shipping large B2B orders to advertisers and distributors.

Why aluminum bottles and paper cartons?

Both aluminum bottles and paper cartons offer a sustainable alternative to plastic bottles. Our long-term goal is to transition to 100% hemp cartons for an even greener solution.

Why BPA free?

We choose to use BPA-free materials because BPA contamination can lead to a host of health problems, including hormonal disruption, diabetes, and high blood pressure.

What charities do you donate to?

Our focus is on contributing to charities that primarily target water-related issues, such as providing clean water, sanitation, and hygiene education to underserved communities. By choosing Balash, you are not just elevating your brand; you are contributing to a cause that aims to improve lives.

How can I help Balash?

The best way to support us is by spreading the word. Share our mission and products on social media and among your friends and family.

How can I distribute Balash?

Our bottles are perfect giveaways for business owners to elevate their brand. They can be distributed at events, through our connections in the event business, or in targeted geographic locations to focus on specific markets. We also offer corporate giveaways, pop-up stands, and influencer campaigns as additional distribution channels. Moreover, vending machines that distribute Balash bottles will be coming soon.
